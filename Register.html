WINA is proud to present its Wellness for International Students program, sponsored by the Canadian Red Cross. This program aims to increase the awareness and education in developing self-protection strategies, developing healthy coping techniques, sustaining life balance, engaging in sports and recreational activities, and building a diverse toolkit for healthy decision making and future choices. Features of the program include a virtual information session conducted by health professional(s) as well as registered participants receiving gift baskets / care packages which will be packed with essentials. These essentials include the likes of hand sanitizer, hand soap, wipes, temperature sensors, masks, feminine hygiene packs and more. The information you provide in the registration form is kept strickly confidential to WINA. We take your privacy very seriously and will not disclose any confidential information to any other source or purpose. *
