OUR GOAL

Wina is an organization passionate about helping newcomers find success and security in Canada with fun events and essential services that not only help them survive but thrive.Since its inception, WINA has evolved over the past 15 years from simply organizing Annual Newcomer Students’ BBQs to now respond to the challenges of international newcomer students in the Halifax Regional Municipality (HRM); to ensure students' success and their integration in Canada.
